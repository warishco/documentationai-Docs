# Waricha Writing Manual

## What this manual is

This repository is **my technical writing manual**.

It’s a playbook of **how I plan, write, review, publish, and maintain technical
content**.

It's a place where I capture decisions, templates, and checklists as reusable
assets.

It's a living document. So expect changes as I learn and refine the system.

### What it covers

- My end-to-end writing process for technical content that educates like
  knowledge base articles, tutorials, and documentation.
- Tools and stack
- Frameworks I frequently use
- Ways of working (collaboration and feedback)

### What it does not cover

Other types of content, such as:

- Marketing copywriting (focused on persuasion)
- Long-form personal essays (focused on expression)

However, some of the principles and practices I describe here can be applied to
other types of content.

## What it is for

- **To make my process visible and repeatable.**
- **To showcase my thinking and writing process when I work with clients.**
- **To share guidance for other technical writers.**

## Who it’s for

- Me (default audience) to document my own technical writing process and
  reference it when I need to
- Content managers, content leads, or founders who want to understand my
  thinking and writing process
- Other technical writers who look for a writing system to adapt
